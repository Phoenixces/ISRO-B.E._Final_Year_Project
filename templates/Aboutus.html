Water Quality Monitoring System

Advanced Monitoring Technologies

Welcome to H2Owebmonitor, a website dedicated to providing accurate and timely water quality predictions. Our mission is to improve public health and protect the environment by providing communities with the tools they need to make informed decisions about water safety.

We use the latest technologies to monitor the quality of lake water in real-time. Our system integrates remote sensing, machine learning, and image processing to provide accurate and reliable data.

Remote Sensing

Our Remote sensing technology allows us to collect real-time water quality data from satellites This data is analyzed using GEE(Google Earth Engine) to provide accurate, up-to-date information about water quality conditions in your area.

Machine Learning

We use machine learning algorithms to analyze the collected data and identify patterns and trends. This helps us to detect anomalies and predict water quality changes.

Image Processing

Our system uses advanced image processing techniques to extract useful information from the collected images. This includes identifying algae blooms and other contaminants.
